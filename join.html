Get Involved

Whether you want to start a chapter at your school, join an existing one, partner with us, or just learn more about what we do, we'd love to hear from you.

eastsidephilosophy@gmail.com

What starting a chapter looks like

We provide everything you need to bring philosophy to your school:

  • Ready-to-use presentations, toolkits, and discussion guides
  • The ESP brand, logo, and listing on our website
  • A Chapter Starter Guide with step-by-step instructions
  • Ongoing support from the ESP executive team

All you need is 3-5 interested students and a faculty advisor. We'll handle the rest.

We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
